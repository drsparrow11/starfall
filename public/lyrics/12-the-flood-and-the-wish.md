# The Flood and the Wish

I know there is a flood
I have seen the water rise
a thousand empty lanterns
thrown into the sky

I know the room gets louder
I know the shelves get filled
I know a song can be released
before the heart is still

But do not blame the first small spark
for every careless flame
do not shame the shaking hand
for learning how to name

There is a flood
and there is a wish
there is a noise
and there is this

There is a way to fill the room
and never hear a thing
there is a way to touch the sky
because you need to sing

Do not confuse
the flood and the wish
do not confuse

Let the first song be crooked

let the strange light flicker
while the maker learns the shape

Let the child of wonder

let the lonely little signal
try to come alive

That is not the drowning
that is not the stain
that is just a human reaching
through a new kind of rain

But when the hands stop listening
when the heart is not involved
when the output does not matter
only numbers, only volume

drowning out the ones who long

A tool can be abused
and still be sacred
in the hands of someone honest
trying to make it through

A sky can be crowded
and still be holy

is telling the truth
