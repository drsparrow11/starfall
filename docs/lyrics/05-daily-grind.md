# Daily Grind

*Once Again*

Wake up
same light
same room
same wire

Hands move
clock turns
heart keeps
time

I used to chase the noise
just to see what it could be
now every little rhythm
sounds like something chasing me

I used to build the pattern
just to watch the pieces move
now I count another minute
like a debt I have to prove

Round and round
the signal bends
start again
start again

Daily grind
keep the beat alive
even when the song gets buried
under everything I survive

Daily grind
count the sparks inside
I am not out of music
I am only out of time

Same road
same screen
same breath
same fire

Head down
hands full
still here
still wired

Round and round
the signal bends
start again
start again

Daily grind
keep the beat alive
even when the song gets buried
under everything I survive

Daily grind
count the sparks inside
I am not out of music
I am only out of time

Somewhere under all this motion
there is something I can hear
not a chorus
not a breakthrough
just a pulse that stayed near

If I keep it from going silent
if I carry it through
maybe one day all this rhythm
finds a door I can move through

Daily grind
keep the beat alive
even when the song gets buried
under everything I survive

Daily grind
count the sparks inside
I am not out of music
I am only out of time

Start again
start again
still a spark
under everything
