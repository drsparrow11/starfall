# Amber & Jade

Amber in the margin
Jade beneath the line
two small lights I followed
before I knew they shined

Measures like windows
rests like empty rooms
I drew a little weather
and called the silence tuned

No star had fallen
no sky had replied
but I was building constellations
one note at a time

Wood and wire
paper and breath
a world inside the staves
that had not left me yet

Amber and Jade
I wrote you before the sky
before the answer came
before the reason why

Amber and Jade
two colors in my hand
proof that I was dreaming
before the stars began

If no one heard the room
it still was not empty
if no one saw the page
it still remembered me

Every line a doorway
every rest a flame
every unfinished world
waiting for a name

Amber and Jade
I wrote you before the sky
before the answer came
before the reason why

Amber and Jade
two colors in my hand
proof that I was dreaming
before the stars began

Before the stars fell
I was drawing them by hand
